Hackathon Hawk
Hackathon Hawk is a simple-to-use mobile friendly web application which allows hackathon enthusiasts to search for upcoming hackathons events.

Not just that, member users can bookmark hackathons of their interest and can then follow updates posted by the hackathon organizers.

Hackathon Hawk is a platform for socializing too. Hackers can search for others hacker members who use Hackathon Hawk, view their profiles and follow them.

On the other hand, Hackathon organizers can setup organizer accounts and post their hackathon. Additionally, they can post updates about their hackathon, which will be visible to all the users who bookmark their hackathon.
